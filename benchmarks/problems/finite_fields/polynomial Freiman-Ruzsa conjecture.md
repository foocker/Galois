Problem.

Let $p$ be a fixed prime and let $A \subseteq \mathbb{F}_p^n$ satisfy
$$
|A+A| \le K|A|.
$$
Must there exist a subspace $H \le \mathbb{F}_p^n$ with $|H| \le |A|$ such that $A$ can be covered by at most $K^{O(1)}$ cosets of $H$?

Context.

This is the polynomial Freiman-Ruzsa conjecture in the finite-field model. It is a standard open problem in additive combinatorics and remains a central reference point in modern surveys of finite-field methods.

Benchmark formulation.

Given a small-doubling set $A \subseteq \mathbb{F}_p^n$, find explicit low-complexity additive structure, or improve the quantitative dependence on $K$ in known covering theorems.
