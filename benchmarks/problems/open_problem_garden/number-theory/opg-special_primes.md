# Statement

Conjecture Let $ p $ be a prime natural number. Find all primes $ q\equiv1\left(\mathrm{mod}\: p\right) $ , such that $ 2^{\frac{\left(q-1\right)}{p}}\equiv1\left(\mathrm{mod}\: q\right) $ .
