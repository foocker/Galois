# Progress

- Primitive pythagorean n-tuple is a n-tuple $ (a_{1},a_{2},a_{3},...,a_{n}) $ such that $ a_{1}^2 + a_{2}^2 + a_{3}^2 + ... + a_{n-1}^2 = a_{n}^2 $

and the greatest common divisor of $ (a_{1},a_{2},a_{3},...,a_{n}) $ is 1.

There are at least two known linear transformation construction of primitive pythagorean triple tree!
