# Statement

Conjecture Let $ E/K $ be an elliptic curve over a number field $ K $ . Then the order of the zeros of its $ L $ -function, $ L(E, s) $ , at $ s = 1 $ is the Mordell-Weil rank of $ E(K) $ .
